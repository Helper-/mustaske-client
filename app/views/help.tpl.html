Mustaske is an application to facilitate asking questions in a group forum.

Join/Create Room

Professor/Creator

  1. If you are a professor/creator simply put in a room name and tap create.
  2. Once in the room give your students or audience members the room id located on the top right or in the settings menu.

Student/Audience

  1. If you are a student or audience member please ask your professor or room owner to give you the room id.
  2. The room id should come in the form adjective-noun-number for example: smelly-pussycat-69'

Navigation

Recent Questions
Under this tab you can find the most recent questions.
Top Questions
On this tab you can find the the top 5 upvoted questions.
Polls
This tab allows you to interact with polls (starting polls for a room owner and voting on polls for audience).
Setting
Under this tab you can find information about this room.
